## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Problem Statement
In **June 2025**, QuickBite faced a major crisis:  

- A **viral social media incident** highlighting food safety violations at partner restaurants  
- A **week-long delivery outage** during the monsoon season  

These events triggered:  

- Significant **customer churn** and disengagement  
- Sharp declines in **daily orders**  
- Dropping **customer satisfaction scores**  
- Loss of **partner restaurants** to competitors  
- Increased **customer acquisition costs**  

QuickBite has allocated a recovery budget, overhauled **food safety protocols**, and upgraded its **delivery infrastructure**. Management expects actionable insights to guide a **strategic recovery**.
